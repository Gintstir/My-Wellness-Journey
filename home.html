Build a burn in 2 minutes or less

  • Plank Variations 1 minute:
    Start in the uppermost position of a push up. Make this easier by lowering your knees to the ground while maintaining a long back. Bring on the burn by bringing your forearms to the ground so that your elbows are under your shoulders. Relax your neck (as much as possible) and hold your chosen position until you can't anymore.
  • Arms in T 45 seconds:
    From standing neutral, bring your arms out to your side palms down. Make this easier by bending your arms at a 90 degree angle from your elbows (palms will still face down). After a few breaths you can rotate your forearms so that your palms face forward or up. for an additional stretch. Try to hold for 5 deep breaths, then release.
